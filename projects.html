Campus Orientation - Interactiv Map

This project was conducted in collaboration with Linnekåren, who desired a website to help new students locate places on campus. The process included:

  • Conducting interviews to understand the needs and desires of the users.
  • Organizing the information based on the data gathered.
  • Designing and programming an interactive website with clickable markers for detailed information about various locations.

Golf mobile App

This app was developed to help golfers in Småland and Öland effortlessly locate golf courses. With a user-friendly interface and real-time API integration, it provides essential course details and availability. The app's design was informed by feedback from local golfers, ensuring it meets their specific needs effectively.
